INICIO COMPETICIONES CONTACTO FAQs

How can I register for the tournaments?

With every new competition we will provide a new link to register. Then you’ll have to fill the form to sign up and your access will be confirmed with a verification email.

Do I have to pay to participate?

The tournaments are usually free, but it could be that some of them need a register payment, which will increase the tournament prize.

Do we get a refund, if there is any problem?

If the problems are alien to the organization, we don’t retorn the money. But, if there is any organisations problem, will make a full refund.

In 5 players-games, which is the team-members limit?

Each team can include 5 players and a maximum of 3 reserve players, who must be in the registration form.

Can you play when you are underage?

Yes, there is no age limit. Only for the on-site games is an age of 16 or more required.

If I’ve paid the registration but for any reason I’m not able to join the competition, is a refund possible?

No. If the registration is already closed and the tournament about to start, we can’t refund the payment.
