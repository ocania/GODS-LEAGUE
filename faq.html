How can I register for the tournaments?

When we present a new competition, we will facilitate the registration link. Only you will have to complete the form and you will receive a verification e-mail with the access confirmation.

Do I have to pay to participate?

Usually, the tournaments are free, but it may be that the registration of some competition is for a fee to increase the prize pool.

In case of problems, do you return the money?

If the problems are alien to the organization, we don’t retorn the money. But, if the organization is the responsable, we will make a full repayment.
